About

I am a psychology researcher interested in computational mechanisms of adaptive decision making. My current work focuses on volatility estimation, belief updating, and choice behavior in Type-I bipolar disorder.

Research

Volatility inference

Modeling how people infer environmental change and update beliefs during uncertain decision tasks.

Belief updating in BD

Testing whether altered volatility beliefs and inflexible updating underlie aberrant decision making in Type-I bipolar disorder.

Computational modeling

Using hierarchical Bayesian models, HGF, reinforcement learning, and model comparison to explain behavioral patterns.
